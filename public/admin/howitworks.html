CCS Office Smartlight Wireless Switch Controller and Monitoring system with ESP8266 Integration

How it Works

  • Power On the Hardware:
    • Locate the power button on the hardware device.
    • Press to turn on the device.
  • Connect to Hardware Hotspot:
    • Once the hardware is powered on, it will emit a hotspot signal.
    • On your device, go to Wi-Fi settings and connect to the hotspot produced by the hardware.
  • Access Captive Portal:
    • Open a web browser on your device.
    • Upon connecting to the hardware hotspot, a captive portal page should automatically appear.
  • View Available Access Points:
    • The captive portal will display a list of available access points.
    • Select the desired Wi-Fi network from the list.
  • Input Wi-Fi Credentials:
    • Enter the credentials (SSID and password) of the selected Wi-Fi network.
    • Confirm the information and proceed.
  • Wait for Hardware Restart:
    • After entering the credentials, wait for the hardware to restart.
    • The device will automatically attempt to connect to the specified Wi-Fi network.
  • Automatic Wi-Fi Connection:
    • Once the hardware restarts, it will automatically connect to the Wi-Fi network using the provided credentials.
  • Hotspot Disappearance:
    • After a successful connection, the hardware hotspot will disappear from the available Wi-Fi networks.
  • Open Web Application:
    • Launch a web browser on your device.
  • Login to Web Application:
    • Enter the web application's URL in the browser.
    • Log in using the appropriate credentials.
  • Homepage
    • Click View to specific room to control.
    • Switch the toggle switch to On or Off any device
  • User Information:
    • Click Edit to change the information of the users
    • Click Blocked to deny access of any users
    • Click Accept to give access of new users
  • User Logs:
    • Click View Logs to view the logs users and can print the logs for hard copy
